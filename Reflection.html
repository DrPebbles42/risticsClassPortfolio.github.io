Reflection

This class was a learning curve considering it is the first completely online class that I have ever taken. Before this, I was in hybrid or completely in person courses. This time, asyncronous learning was the backbone of the course.

Group projects were also huge during this class which was also something to get used to, but it was great to see everyone put in their effort and best work considering that we never met up for a call or anything and we still made some amazing projects.

Overall, this course was something of a refresher for a lot of the information I already knew. It gave me another solid foundational class that went in detail without going too far in detail. The collaboration was awesome, and that was definitely my favorite part of the class.
