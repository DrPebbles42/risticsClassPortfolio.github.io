    • Week 1: Course Introduction

    Beginning the semester, we participated in a discussion to reflect on what work style we are. There was a video to watch to learn about the workstyles found on LinkedIn here. The work styles included were:

    • Prioritizers: Logical, Analytical, and Data Oritented. They ask the "What?" questions.
    • Planners: Organized, Detail Oriented, Do things in a sequence. They ask the "How?" questions to understand the process.
    • Arrangers: Expressive, Emotional, Supportive. They ask the "Who?" questions to understand who is involved and what their roles are.
    • Visualizers: The big picture, creative people. They tend to ask the "Why?" questions. They want to understand how everything fits and works together.
    I identified with the prioritizer and arranger role at the beginning of the course, but I now feel as though I fit most in with the "Visualizer" work style the longer I've work in tech.
